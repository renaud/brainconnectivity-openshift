Matrix of connections

Matrices of connection counts between all regions. Note that the frequency of a brain region connection reported in scientific articles does not necessarily reflect the physiological intensity of a connection; the former reflecting the ’popularity’ of a region.

  • matrix with top 30x30 regions: ABA, braiNER
  • matrix with top 100x100 regions: ABA, braiNER
  • matrix with top 300x300 regions: ABA, braiNER (slow)
  • matrix with all regions: ABA, braiNER (VERY slow)

REST service

Above pages are also available as a REST service (JSON)

  • ABA matrix, braiNER matrix
    url format: /{db}/br/matrix?size={size}
  • ABA region, braiNER region
    url format: /{db}/br/region/{region_id}
  • ABA details, braiNER details
    url format: /{db}/br/details/{region_id1}/{region_id2}
  • search
    url format: /search/{query_string}
